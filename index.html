SOLIHULL WEDDING PLANNER LTD

Solihull Wedding Planner Ltd is an award-winning independent UK Wedding Planner. Based in Warwickshire, in the heart of the West Midlands, we plan, design and coordinate personal and individually tailored weddings that will leave you smiling from ear to ear. From traditional to vintage, intimate to grand, each wedding we do is very different and we love them all, no matter how big or small.
